# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 08: The Dark Wars & the Sith Triumvirate (≈3,955–3,950 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Dark Wars** that followed the Jedi Civil War — the near-extermination of the Jedi Order (the **First Jedi Purge**) by the **Sith Triumvirate** of Darth Traya, Darth Nihilus, and Darth Sion, and the journey of the reawakened **Jedi Exile, Meetra Surik**, who unraveled and destroyed them. This is the heart of *Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords*. Drawn from KOTOR II (Obsidian), *The Old Republic: Revan*, and supporting Legends sources.

This doc delivers the **reawakened Meetra Surik** sheet promised from Era 06 (her wartime and Force-severed states are noted there).

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **Intelligence** — **Darth Traya (Kreia)** is the era's legendary manipulator-philosopher (102); the engineer **Bao-Dur** scores very high. **Darth Nihilus** sits near the bottom — he is barely a mind anymore, just hunger.
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude. **This era is the showcase stat for Nihilus**, whose planet-devouring Force-drain is *pure* Output (112, legendary) even as his Intelligence and dueling are unremarkable.

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every stat block (this sheet predated the v3 stats).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** all seven classic forms are codified. Each duelist carries numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-saber fighters show *(none)* with a melee/ranged rating noted.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** each registry ability at base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`), with notable sub-ability proficiency percentages 0–100 in parentheses (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (planetary Force-devour, pain-fueled immortality, Force-bond mastery, mind-folding) flagged separately.

### Era Notes on Scaling
The Triumvirate are **specialist legendary** Sith — each terrifying in a single, narrow dimension rather than across the board, which fits the per-stat rule perfectly. **Darth Nihilus** is a near-mindless legendary *Force-devourer* (consumes the life of entire planets) but an ordinary duelist with almost no Intelligence. **Darth Sion** is a legendary *unkillable* pain-engine, a brutal duelist who cannot be killed by conventional means. **Darth Traya/Kreia** is a legendary *manipulator and Force philosopher* whose raw combat is only Master-tier. **Meetra Surik** is a unique legendary *connection-forger*. This era is a showcase for "legendary in one thing, mortal in others."

### Multi-sheet payoff
- **Meetra Surik** gets her **reawakened Jedi Master** sheet here (wartime & Force-severed states are in Era 06).
- **Kreia / Darth Traya** is one sheet (same being, same stats, differing only in revealed alignment).

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Darth Traya (Kreia)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, head of the Triumvirate; the Lord of Betrayal
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (female) · Jedi Order → Sith Triumvirate · Jedi Master/Historian → Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** A Jedi Master before the Mandalorian Wars (one of Revan's teachers); fell and became Darth Traya; betrayed and exiled by her own apprentices; reclaimed the title; died at Malachor V (3,950 BBY)
- **Summary:** Once **Kreia**, a contemplative Jedi Master, historian, and one of Revan's teachers, she was exiled by the Council when her students followed Revan to war. Retracing Revan's path to Malachor V, she fell and became **Darth Traya**, gathering the Force-wounds Sion and Nihilus as apprentices to form the Triumvirate — only to be betrayed and cast out by them, then reclaim her mantle. Her true goal was singular and heretical: she had come to **hate the Force itself**, and sought to kill it, to free the galaxy from its will. A master manipulator who guided and tested Meetra Surik even while opposing her. Her combat is Master-tier; her true weapons are knowledge, prophecy, and betrayal.

## Darth Nihilus — the Lord of Hunger
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, a living wound in the Force
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (former; now a robed void) · Sith Triumvirate · Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** A Mandalorian-Wars survivor of Malachor V; rose to power in the Dark Wars; destroyed at the Battle of Telos IV (3,951 BBY)
- **Summary:** Once a being who lost everything at Malachor V, he survived the Mass Shadow Generator's detonation — an experience that transformed him into a **wound in the Force** with an insatiable hunger to consume Force energy. Taught by Kreia to feed that hunger, he became **Darth Nihilus, the Lord of Hunger** — barely a person anymore, just a robed void of need that drains the life and Force from everything around it. He consumed the entire Force-sensitive population of Katarr in a single act, and his mere presence aboard his flagship *Ravager* killed his own crew. His power to devour is legendary and world-ending — but he is so hollowed-out that he is a less refined duelist than his raw power suggests, and vulnerable to those (like Surik) immune to his hunger.

## Darth Sion — the Lord of Pain
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, the immortal corpse-warrior
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (former; now a reanimated ruin) · Sith Triumvirate · Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** An ancient Sith warrior who died many times; active through the Dark Wars; finally let himself die at Malachor V (3,950 BBY)
- **Summary:** An ancient Sith warrior whose body is **literally in pieces** — every bone broken, muscle torn, skin scorched and rotted — kept alive and held together by nothing but his **hatred and pain channeled through the dark side**. He feels agony every waking moment and turns it into power and an essentially **unkillable** body: cut him down and his rage reassembles him. Trained by Kreia, he became **Darth Sion, the Lord of Pain**, a brutal duelist obsessed with destroying the Jedi. He hunted Surik across the galaxy and could only be defeated by breaking his *will* to keep suffering.

## Meetra Surik — the Jedi Exile (reawakened)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — reawakened Jedi Master, the Wound in the Force made whole
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (female) · Jedi Order · General (former) → reawakened Jedi Master
- **Lifespan:** Severed from the Force at Malachor V (3,960 BBY); reconnected in 3,951 BBY; destroyed the Triumvirate and survived; killed at Dromund Kaas in 3,950 BBY (stabbed by Lord Scourge while attacking the Sith Emperor) — endured afterward as a Force ghost
- **Summary:** The only Jedi who went to war under Revan, severed her own Force connection at Malachor V, and returned **neither fallen nor dead**. For nearly a decade she lived as a "wound in the Force" — which paradoxically made her *immune* to Nihilus's hunger and able to form unusually deep, conscious Force bonds. Drawn back into events by Kreia and hunted by the Triumvirate, she slowly **reconnected to the Force**, retrained herself and her companions as Jedi, and one by one unmade the three Sith Lords. Her signature is not raw power but **connection**: she forges Force bonds, teaches, and empowers those around her, turning ordinary people into Jedi.

- **Signature Ability:** **Force-bond mastery** — an unmatched gift for forging deep Force connections, empowering allies, and awakening latent Force-sensitives; her wound-in-the-Force nature makes her immune to Force-drain.
- **Notable Feats:** Activated the Mass Shadow Generator at Malachor V; survived as a wound in the Force; reconnected and retrained herself; redeemed Visas Marr; broke Darth Sion's will; destroyed Darth Nihilus (immune to his hunger); confronted Darth Traya at Malachor; trained a new circle of Jedi.
- **Alignment:** Light side.
- **Tier:** **Legendary** (Force-bond / empowerment specialist); elite duelist; Master-tier overall Force.
