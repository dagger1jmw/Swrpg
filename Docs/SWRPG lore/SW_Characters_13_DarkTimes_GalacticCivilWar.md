# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets

## Era 13: The Dark Times \& the Galactic Civil War (≈19 BBY – 4 ABY)

\---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Dark Times** (the Jedi Purge and the long Imperial night) and the **Galactic Civil War** (the Rebellion against the Empire, ending at the Battle of Endor, 4 ABY). This is the **original trilogy era**: **Darth Vader** as the Emperor's enforcer, **Emperor Palpatine** ruling openly, **Ben Kenobi** and **Yoda** in exile, the rise of **Luke Skywalker**, and the Empire's dark-side servants — Inquisitors, Emperor's Hands, and Vader's secret apprentice **Galen Marek (Starkiller)**. It also pays off the long-flagged reawakening of **Arden Lyn** (Era 02). Drawn from the original trilogy, the *Force Unleashed* games, *Dark Forces/Jedi Knight*, the *Dark Times* comics, and supporting Legends sources.

The Emperor's \\\\\\\*\\\\\\\*Dark Empire clone-resurrection\\\\\\\*\\\\\\\* is a \\\\\\\*later\\\\\\\* era (\\\\\\\~10–11 ABY, Era 14); this sheet is his living, pre-Endor self. \\\\\\\*\\\\\\\*Thrawn's\\\\\\\*\\\\\\\* prime is post-Endor (Era 14) — flagged, not statted here.

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)

Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### The Force block — five stats (Output vs Control)

**ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · ForceOutput.** Output = *raw power/magnitude*; Control = *precision/finesse*. This era is full of clean Output↔Control divergences (Vader, Galen Marek, the aged exiles).

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)

**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** the five above.

* **Intelligence** — the Emperor (100, supreme manipulator), the brilliant Leia (90), the elder sages Yoda (96) and Ben (90), and the tactician Tarkin (84) top the era.

### Apex-ladder application (per Era 09 APEX CEILING)

* **Emperor Palpatine (living)** now rises into the **Tier A apex cluster (\~130 ceiling)** — the apex realization of the Rule of Two, the most powerful living Sith of the modern age. Below Vitiate (\~134), Krayt (\~132), and peak Luke (\~138); above everyone else in this era. His **Dark Empire clone** (Era 14) climbs higher still, but stays below peak Luke.
* **The Output↔Control inversion (Anakin → Vader):**

  * **Knightfall Anakin** (Era 12): Output **120**, Control **90** — the highest *observed* Anakin (still growing), raw/unhoned.
  * **Vader ANH** (here): Output **108**, Control **106** — the suit caps raw power below Knightfall, discipline rises.
  * **Vader RotJ-peak** (here): Output **112**, Control **114** — his most honed and precise, still below his organic-peak output.
* **Galen Marek** is a near-apex *raw-output* prodigy (Output 116) — just under the Chosen One's observed Knightfall peak — with comparatively raw Control (94).
* **Luke** is explicitly *rising / pre-prime*. His apex Grand Master prime (\~138, strongest realized mortal) is **Era 17**, not here.

* **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Galactic Emperor and Dark Lord of the Sith, ruling openly (Tier A apex cluster)

* **Lifespan:** Born \~82 BBY; Chancellor 32 BBY; Emperor 19 BBY; killed at Endor 4 ABY (resurrected via clones later, \~10 ABY)
* **Summary:** The Sith master whose thousand-year Grand Plan came to fruition — now ruling the galaxy openly as **Emperor**, his face disfigured by his own Force lightning (reflected by Mace Windu) into the pale, hooded specter of legend. The same being as the hidden Chancellor (Era 11), no longer concealed: the most accomplished Sith of the modern age, the apex realization of Bane's Rule of Two, a supreme manipulator and a devastating sorcerer whose **Force lightning** could kill almost anything. He kept Vader as his enforcer while always seeking a stronger or younger replacement (Galen Marek, then Luke). His arrogance — certain he could turn or kill Luke at Endor — let the redeemed Vader throw him to his death. An apex being: enormous Output, supreme Control, unmatched Knowledge.

## Darth Vader (I) — ANH-era enforcer

* **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — the Emperor's enforcer and Jedi-hunter, early Imperial era through A New Hope
* **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human cyborg · Sith / Galactic Empire · Dark Lord of the Sith, Supreme Commander of the Imperial Fleet
* **Lifespan:** Entombed in the armor 19 BBY; serves the Emperor through the Dark Times; dies redeemed at Endor 4 ABY
* **Summary:** Anakin Skywalker rebuilt as the cybernetic **Darth Vader** — the black-armored Dark Lord, more machine than man, the Emperor's right hand and the terror of the galaxy. For two decades he hunted down the surviving Jedi (and trained, then was nearly betrayed by, his secret apprentice Galen Marek). The life-support armor cost him much of his organic-peak raw power (his Knightfall **Output 120** is reduced further), but it made him an armored juggernaut of enhanced strength and durability — and over the years his **Control** rose far above his unhoned Knightfall self into cold, precise, surgical mastery. By A New Hope he was somewhat "rusty" from years without a true duel (his slow, measured fight with the aged Ben Kenobi reflects two past-prime warriors), but still vastly dangerous. The colder, more emptied-out Vader — the suppressed shell of Anakin going through the Emperor's work.

## Darth Vader (II) — ESB/RotJ combat peak

* **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Vader at his most honed, The Empire Strikes Back through Return of the Jedi
* **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human cyborg · Sith / Galactic Empire · Dark Lord of the Sith
* **Lifespan:** His combat and Force peak as Vader, \~3–4 ABY; redeemed and dies at Endor 4 ABY
* **Summary:** By the time he hunts the Rebels to Hoth, Bespin, and Endor, Vader is at the **peak of his powers as a suited Sith** — sharper, more active, and more dangerous than his ANH self. The reawakening of Anakin (discovering Luke is his son) reignites something in him, and his dueling reaches its most honed: he dominated Luke at Bespin (toying with him, then taking his hand) and fought him at his most controlled at Endor. His raw **Output (105)** is still capped by the suit below the Knightfall organic peak (120), but his **Control (114)** is at its absolute height — two decades of refinement making him a precise, overwhelming master. His arc ends in redemption: turning on the Emperor to save his son, dying as Anakin Skywalker.

## Galen Marek — "Starkiller," Vader's secret apprentice

* **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Vader's secret apprentice, a near-apex telekinetic prodigy
* **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith (secret, unsanctioned) → redeemed · Dark Jedi apprentice, codename "Starkiller"
* **Lifespan:** Born \~19 BBY on Kashyyyk; trained in secret by Vader; died \~2 BBY redeemed, helping found the Rebel Alliance
* **Summary:** The son of a Jedi, taken as a child by Darth Vader after Vader killed his father — sensing in the boy raw Force power greater than almost anyone alive. Raised in secret as **Starkiller**, Vader's deniable assassin and his intended weapon to overthrow the Emperor, Galen became a **"Force wrecking ball"**: a telekinetic powerhouse who could rip a Star Destroyer from the sky, shatter ranks of troops with a single Repulse, and hunt down Jedi survivors (Rahm Kota, Kazdan Paratus, Shaak Ti) — and who defeated **Vader himself twice**. Palpatine claimed Starkiller's strength in the Force could *potentially rival his own* — Sith flattery and manipulation as much as fact (the Emperor's realized power far exceeded his). Betrayed by both Sith, Galen turned to the light, helped unite the founders of the Rebel Alliance, and died confronting the Emperor to let them escape — a martyr-symbol of the Rebellion. His defining trait is colossal raw **Output** with comparatively raw, aggressive Control.
