# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 14: The New Republic & the Rise of the New Jedi Order (≈4–25 ABY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **New Republic era** — from the Battle of Endor (4 ABY) through the rebuilding of galactic government and the founding of Luke Skywalker's **New Jedi Order**, up to the eve of the Yuuzhan Vong War (~25 ABY). It covers the **Thrawn campaign** (9 ABY), the **Dark Empire** clone-Emperor crisis (~10–11 ABY), Luke's **Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4** (and the return of **Exar Kun's spirit**), the redemption of **Mara Jade**, and the first generation of new Jedi. Drawn from the *Thrawn Trilogy*, *Dark Empire*, the *Jedi Academy* trilogy, *I, Jedi*, *Hand of Thrawn*, the *Jedi Knight* games, and supporting Legends sources.

> **Continuity note (Legends):** Core Legends. Post-Endor the Empire fractured into warlord fiefs before consolidating into the **Imperial Remnant** under Pellaeon. The **reborn Emperor** (cloned bodies, Dark Empire) is the same being as the living Palpatine (Eras 11/13) — finally destroyed permanently ~11 ABY by Empatojayos Brand. The **Solo twins** (Jacen, Jaina) and **Anakin Solo** are born/children here; their primes are Eras 15–16. **Thrawn dies** at Bilbringi (9 ABY), assassinated by Rukh.

Stats represent each character **at their prime for this era**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** See the **APEX CEILING** in Era 09 for the 126–150 sub-bands. |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl (finesse) · ForceOutput (raw power).
- **Intelligence** — this era's flagship case is **Thrawn (112)**: a non-Force being who is "legendary" purely through intellect and command. The reborn Emperor (108) and the broker Talon Karrde / leaders (84–90) also score high.
- **ForceOutput vs ForceControl** still diverge — **Kyp Durron** is the poster child (huge Output 110, lagging Control 88).

### Apex-ladder application (per Era 09 APEX CEILING)
- **The reborn Emperor (Dark Empire clone)** is his **most unleashed form** — open, reckless, channeling planet-scale Force storms. He climbs to the **top of the Tier A apex cluster (~135)**: above his living self (Era 13, ~130), still **below peak Luke (~138)**. His fleet-destroying Force Storm is rated as a specialist ability (~138).
- **Luke is ascending, NOT yet at apex.** Here he is the most powerful Jedi alive at **Output 118 / Control 108** — but in *this* era he is still **below the reborn Emperor (135)**, which is exactly why he briefly fell and needed Empatojayos Brand's sacrifice to end the Emperor. His true apex (**~138, strongest realized mortal, second only to Abeloth**) peaks in **Era 17**.

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric forms & abilities
- **Form ratings (game stats):** numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-Force fighters show *(none)* / a combat rating. (Intelligence and ForceOutput were already present from this era's original build.)
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (Force-storm/spirit-transfer, mind-rewriting, the Halcyon absorption-into-telekinesis trick) flagged separately.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Luke Skywalker (New Republic — founder/Master)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime (this era) — the most powerful Jedi alive, founder of the New Jedi Order (ascending toward his apex)
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · New Jedi Order · Jedi Master, founder and head of the Order
- **Lifespan:** Born 19 BBY; founds the Praxeum ~11 ABY; the New Order's Grand Master through this era and beyond
- **Summary:** The galaxy's lone fully-fledged Jedi after Endor, Luke spent this era reconstructing the Order almost from scratch — recovering lost lore, founding the **Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4**, and training the first generation. He faced the insane clone **Joruus C'baoth**, and during the **Dark Empire** crisis deliberately submitted to the reborn Emperor to learn the dark side from within — briefly becoming his apprentice before Leia pulled him back, a near-fall that taught him the path's dangers. He survived Exar Kun's spirit cleaving his soul from his body (restored by his students), married **Mara Jade**, and by the end of the era was unambiguously the **most powerful and accomplished Jedi of his age** — though his ultimate apex is still ahead. His Control and Knowledge especially climbed steeply across these years.

## Kyp Durron
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — raw-power prodigy of the new Order (fell, redeemed)
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · New Jedi Order · student → Jedi (later Master)
- **Lifespan:** Imprisoned on Kessel as a child; rescued ~11 ABY; fell under Exar Kun, redeemed; rises to Jedi Master
- **Summary:** A young man of **staggering raw Force potential** — when Luke tested him, the feedback threw Luke across the room. Rescued from the Kessel spice mines, he became the Praxeum's strongest student but chafed at Luke's caution, and the spirit of **Exar Kun** seized on his anger: under Kun's influence Kyp stole the **Sun Crusher** superweapon, blasted Luke's soul from his body, and destroyed the Carida system (killing millions, including his own brother) before Kun's spirit was destroyed and Kyp was freed. Wracked with guilt, he sought atonement, and — given a second chance — grew into one of the New Order's most powerful (and most controversial) Masters. His defining trait is colossal **Output** outrunning his **Control**.

## Corran Horn
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — CorSec investigator/Rogue Squadron ace turned Jedi Master
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · New Republic (Rogue Squadron) / New Jedi Order · CorSec investigator → pilot → Jedi Knight → Master
- **Lifespan:** Active through the New Republic era; grandson of Jedi Master Nejaa Halcyon
- **Summary:** A former Corellian Security investigator and ace Rogue Squadron pilot, grandson of the Jedi Nejaa Halcyon, who came late to his Force heritage. Trained at Luke's Praxeum, he was instrumental in destroying Exar Kun's temple/spirit. The **Halcyon/Horn line has a distinctive Force profile**: they are **weak-to-nonexistent in raw telekinesis** (Corran can barely make a stone wobble unaided) — but **exceptionally strong in mind tricks, illusions, and mental projection** (implanting thoughts, sensations, images), and gifted with **Tutaminis (energy absorption)**. Cleverly, Corran can **absorb energy — blaster bolts, lightsaber blades, even heat — and channel it into temporary telekinetic feats** that would otherwise be beyond him (as his grandfather did). A sharp investigator's mind, principled and disciplined; he became a respected Jedi Master.

## Emperor Palpatine (reborn — Dark Empire)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — the resurrected Emperor in cloned bodies, at the top of the Tier A apex cluster
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (clone bodies) · resurgent Sith Empire / Dark Empire · Emperor; Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** Killed at Endor (Era 13, 4 ABY); consciousness transferred into clone bodies on Byss; rampages ~10–11 ABY; destroyed permanently by Empatojayos Brand
- **Summary:** Palpatine cheated death: his spirit, prepared for just this, transferred into a series of **cloned bodies** hidden on the Deep Core world of **Byss**, returning during the Dark Empire crisis to nearly reconquer the galaxy. Wielding immense dark-side power — vast **Force storms** capable of devastating fleets and worlds — he briefly turned **Luke Skywalker** into his apprentice and sought a young Solo child as a permanent host. His clone bodies degraded under the strain of channeling so much dark power, forcing repeated transfers. He was finally destroyed *permanently* when the dying Jedi **Empatojayos Brand** bound the Emperor's spirit to his own and dragged it into the netherworld of the Force. This is his **most unleashed incarnation** — even more reckless with raw power than his living self.

## Grand Admiral Thrawn
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — the Empire's greatest military mind
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Chiss · Imperial Remnant · Grand Admiral, Supreme Commander of Imperial forces
- **Lifespan:** Rose under Palpatine; led the Imperial resurgence 9 ABY; assassinated at Bilbringi by Rukh
- **Summary:** The blue-skinned, red-eyed Chiss tactical genius who emerged from the Unknown Regions to nearly reverse the entire Galactic Civil War through sheer strategic brilliance. Studying a culture's *art* to predict its behavior, Thrawn out-thought the New Republic at every turn, recovered the Emperor's cloning technology at Mount Tantiss, enlisted the mad clone Joruus C'baoth, and pushed the Republic to the brink — until his Noghri bodyguard **Rukh**, whose people his Empire had betrayed, assassinated him at Bilbringi. **Not Force-sensitive** — his power is entirely intellect, will, and command — but one of the most dangerous individuals of the era regardless. He used **ysalamiri** (Force-repelling creatures) to neutralize enemy Jedi.
