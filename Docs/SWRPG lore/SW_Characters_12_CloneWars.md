# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 12: The Clone Wars (≈22–19 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Clone Wars** — the galaxy-spanning war between the Galactic Republic and the Separatist Confederacy that Darth Sidious engineered as the final act of his Grand Plan, ending with **Order 66**, the fall of the Jedi, and the birth of the Empire (19 BBY). This is where **Anakin Skywalker** rises as the Hero With No Fear and falls to become **Darth Vader**. Drawn from *Attack of the Clones*, *Revenge of the Sith*, the *Clone Wars* (2003 Tartakovsky) and *The Clone Wars* (2008) material, the *Labyrinth of Evil* / *Revenge of the Sith* novelizations, and supporting Legends sources.

> **Continuity note (Legends):** This follows **core Legends**. **Order 66 in Legends was carried out through clone loyalty, indoctrination, and conditioning — there were NO inhibitor chips** (that is a Disney-canon device). Anakin's prime here is his Knight self and his fall; his **Darth Vader suit incarnations** (ANH-shell and RotJ-peak) are in **Era 13**.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** See the **APEX CEILING** in Era 09 for the 126–150 sub-bands. |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### The Force block — five stats (Output vs Control)
**ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · ForceOutput.** Output = *raw power/magnitude*; Control = *precision/finesse*. The clearest example is **Anakin**: his **Knightfall** self has high-legendary Output (120, and still growing) but only high-Master Control (90 — raw, unhoned), while suited Vader (Era 13) inverts it: lower Output, far higher Control.

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** the five above.
- **Intelligence** — the Separatist statesman **Dooku** (90) and the diplomat-strategist **Obi-Wan** ("the Negotiator," 88) top the era; **Anakin** is a brilliant tactician/pilot/mechanic (84) but reckless; **Grievous** a cunning-but-cowardly tactician (76).

### Era Notes on Scaling
The Clone Wars **war-tempered** the surviving prequel Jedi. The standouts are now genuinely legendary: **Anakin** (most powerful Jedi of the age), **Obi-Wan** (legendary Soresu master), **Dooku/Tyranus** (legendary Makashi + lightning). **Grievous** is a legendary *non-Force* duelist. Yoda, Mace Windu, and Sidious carry their legendary tiers from Era 11.

### Apex-ladder application (per Era 09 APEX CEILING)
- **Knightfall Anakin** reaches **ForceOutput 120 — high legendary**, sitting *below* the Tier A apex cluster (Vitiate ~134, Krayt ~132). This is the **highest level ever observed from Anakin**, but he was **still growing and not at full strength**. His *theoretical* full potential (~138 — the ceiling only Luke ever freely realized) was never reached; his Control also lags (90).
- On **Mortis**, the Force-nexus realm briefly *revealed* that latent potential — letting him approach ~138 and subdue the Son — but that was the realm unlocking his ceiling, not his normal real-world level (Knightfall ~120). The ruling holds: a near-full-potential Chosen One overcame the Son, so **realized-peak Luke ≥ the Mortis Ones**, with **Abeloth the sole clear superior to peak Luke.**

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + Intelligence
- **Intelligence** added to every block (Output was already present from this era's original build).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-Force fighters show *(none)* / a combat rating.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Anakin Skywalker (I) — Jedi Knight, the Hero With No Fear
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Jedi Knight and General, the Chosen One at his heroic height
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order / Republic Military · Jedi Knight, General
- **Lifespan:** Born 41 BBY on Tatooine; knighted during the Clone Wars; fell to the dark side 19 BBY
- **Summary:** The prophesied **Chosen One**, destined to bring balance to the Force, and the most naturally powerful Force-user of his generation. Trained by Obi-Wan, knighted early in the war, he became the galaxy-famed **"Hero With No Fear"** — a brilliant, daring General and starfighter ace whose raw power and dueling outstripped nearly every Jedi alive. He took **Ahsoka Tano** as his Padawan. A master of **Form V (Djem So)**, he killed Count Dooku and ended battle after battle — but his fierce attachment and fear of loss were the cracks through which Sidious destroyed him. At his Jedi peak his power was already approaching legendary apex; only his youth, recklessness, unresolved fear — and his own Jedi restraint — kept him from the full mastery the prophecy promised.

## Anakin Skywalker (II) — Knightfall / the fall to Darth Vader
- **Sheet snapshot:** Fall prime — newly turned Sith apprentice "Darth Vader," walls down, full power unleashed (pre-suit)
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith (Sidious's apprentice) · Dark Lord of the Sith (Darth Vader)
- **Lifespan:** Fell 19 BBY; maimed on Mustafar and entombed in the Vader armor (Era 13)
- **Summary:** Manipulated through his terror of losing Padmé, Anakin pledged himself to Sidious as **Darth Vader**. This is the single most dangerous version of Anakin who ever lived — *in raw output*. As a Jedi he had **always held back**; at Knightfall the walls came down and his Force *output* surged into the high-legendary band. Leading the assault on the Jedi Temple (Operation: Knightfall), he cut through the Order's defenders — slaying **Cin Drallig**, the Order's Battlemaster, and his students; the security hologram shows him dueling and killing the Temple's finest blade while casually Force-choking another Jedi at the same time. His *control* did **not** keep pace — raw, unstable, unhoned. Crucially, **he was still growing in power — not yet at full strength.** This is the highest level we ever *observed* from Anakin, but it sits below his theoretical full potential (~138, the ceiling only Luke ever freely realized). He was defeated on Mustafar not by a stronger foe but by his **own arrogance** (the reckless high-ground gamble against Obi-Wan's flawless defense), dismembered and burned, rebuilt as the diminished Vader machine (Era 13). This is the **pre-suit fallen Anakin at full unleashing** — his observed organic peak of raw power.

> **Cross-era note (the Output↔Control inversion):** After Mustafar, the cybernetic **Darth Vader** has a *different profile* — the suit **drastically reduces** his Output ("more machine now than man"), but over decades his **Control rises far above** this Knightfall figure. His **Era 13 Vader sheets** invert this: *lower Output, much higher Control*. He never again reaches this raw-power peak, but becomes a far more disciplined fighter. One of the few cases where a character's earlier incarnation is his most powerful in raw terms.

# THE MORTIS ONES (the embodiments of the Force)

> **Apex-ceiling ruling (read first):** The Father, Son, and Daughter are **abstract embodiments of the Force itself**, bound to the Force-vergence realm of **Mortis**. Their listed figures reflect that cosmic nature, which on Mortis reads in the **Ω band**. **But per the project's APEX CEILING:** on Mortis the realm *briefly revealed* Anakin's latent full potential (~138 — higher than his real-world observed Knightfall peak of ~120), and that near-full-potential Anakin **overcame the Son**. Since the Chosen One's full potential equals what **peak Luke** freely realized, the Ones are treated as **matched/overcome by a realized-peak Chosen One**, i.e. **peak Luke (~138) ≥ the Ones**, with **Abeloth (~145) the sole being clearly above peak Luke.** Treat the high numbers below as *Mortis-bound Force-embodiment* power, not a free-roaming combat ranking above Luke. *(Mortis arc is 2008 Clone Wars material — borderline Legends; included to anchor the apex ruling.)*
