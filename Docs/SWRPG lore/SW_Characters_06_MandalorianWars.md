# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 06: The Mandalorian Wars & the Rise of Revan (≈3,976–3,960 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Mandalorian Wars** — the brutal sixteen-year conflict (3,976–3,960 BBY) in which the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders under Mandalore the Ultimate swept across the Republic, the Jedi Council refused to act, and the Jedi Knight Revan defied them to lead the Republic to victory at the catastrophic Battle of Malachor V. Drawn from *Knights of the Old Republic* (the BioWare game and the Dark Horse comic), *The Old Republic: Revan*, and supporting Legends sources. (Revan and Malak's full *Jedi Civil War* dark-empire arc belongs to Era 07; this doc covers their Mandalorian-Wars and falling-point selves, with their Dark Lord sheets pointing forward.)

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **Intelligence** — this era's defining trait belongs to **Revan**, a once-in-an-age strategic genius (legendary INT). The Mandalorian doctrine-architect **Cassus Fett** also scores very high.
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude (distinct from ForceControl, which is finesse).

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every stat block (this sheet predated the v3 stats).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** by the KotOR era **all seven classic forms are codified and named** (Shii-Cho I, Makashi II, Soresu III, Ataru IV, Shien/Djem So V, Niman VI, Juyo VII). Each duelist now carries numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Forms a character never trained are omitted (= 0). Non-saber fighters show *(none)* with a melee rating noted.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** each registry ability at base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`), with notable sub-ability proficiency percentages 0–100 in parentheses (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (Force connection-forging / -severing) flagged separately.

### Era Notes on Scaling
This era is the high-water mark of the "war-tempered" Old Republic Jedi — Knights forged in combat far exceed peacetime norms. **Revan** is one of the genuine apex duelist-generals of all Legends (legendary in dueling, leadership, Intelligence, and Force breadth). The Mandalorians are at their most dangerous as a culture-wide military machine. Battle meditation, strategic genius, and Echani martial arts are signature capabilities here.

### Multi-sheet characters
**Revan** and **Malak** both fall to the dark side at the *end* of this era (after Malachor V). Their Mandalorian-Wars Jedi sheets are here; their **Darth Revan / Darth Malak** Dark Lord sheets are noted here but fully statted in **Era 07 (Jedi Civil War)**. Revan's later redeemed/amnesiac incarnations are flagged for Era 07+.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Revan (I) — the Revanchist (Jedi Knight / Supreme Commander)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Mandalorian-Wars prime — Jedi Knight, leader of the Revanchists, Supreme Commander of the Republic
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order → Republic Military · Jedi Knight, Supreme Commander
- **Lifespan:** Born ~3,994 BBY; led the Revanchists from ~3,963 BBY; won Malachor V in 3,960 BBY, then fell to the dark side in the Unknown Regions (see Era 07)
- **Summary:** A charismatic, brilliant Jedi Knight and the most vocal critic of the Council's refusal to enter the Mandalorian Wars. Defying the Order, he donned the mask of a fallen Mandalorian, rallied a third of the Jedi to his "Revanchist" banner, and was made Supreme Commander. A strategic genius who turned the Mandalorians' own tactics against them, he liberated Taris, won at Althir, and forced the final confrontation at Malachor V — where he slew Mandalore the Ultimate in single combat and authorized the Mass Shadow Generator. Trained in close combat by the Echani and a master of both single-blade and dual-blade lightsaber forms, he was already one of the finest duelists of the age before his fall. He wielded a violet blade as a Jedi.

Niman fits Revan exactly: a balanced synthesis of all preceding forms with no real weakness, it lets him weave **telekinetic attacks directly into his bladework** (the "draw closer" and "pushing slash") and frees him to develop the broader Force and strategic powers that define him, rather than over-investing in pure swordplay. ## Revan (II) — Darth Revan, Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Sheet snapshot:** *Pointer entry* — fully statted in **Era 07 (Jedi Civil War)**
- **Summary:** After Malachor V, Revan and Malak followed a hidden Sith influence into the Unknown Regions, discovered the reconstituted Sith Empire, and were turned by the Sith Emperor (Vitiate). Breaking free of the Emperor's control, Revan became **Darth Revan, Dark Lord of the Sith**, took Malak as apprentice, seized the Star Forge, and waged the Jedi Civil War. His dark powers added Force lightning and life-drain to his arsenal, and he wielded a crimson blade. *(Full Dark Lord and later redeemed "Prodigal Knight" sheets are in Era 07.)*
- **Cross-era note:** Do not stat here — see Era 07. His Dark Lord power is a lateral/raw-power shift, not a clean upgrade: ForceOutput rises (Force lightning, life-drain) but his already-legendary control/strategy stay comparable.

*Era 06 of the Star Wars Legends character-sheet series. Stats are prime-version, galaxy-wide scale. **Now fully game-ready: ForceOutput + Intelligence added to every block, numeric named-form ratings (all seven classic forms are codified by the KotOR era), and numeric Force Ability stats (base level + sub-ability proficiency %).** Revan's legendary Intelligence (100) reflects his defining trait as the strategic genius who reversed the war.*

> **Note on Revan's primary form (Niman):** Drew Karpyshyn — Revan's creator — stated his view that Revan primarily used Form VI (Niman), as it suits an "intelligent and adaptable" user, has no real weaknesses, and lets the wielder integrate Force powers into combat while developing broader Force/strategic mastery. This is the author's stated opinion rather than a hard in-universe canon line, and official sources also stress Revan's all-forms generalism and Ataru-style acrobatics — but since Niman *is* the adaptable, Force-integrating generalist form, the two framings coincide. His sheet therefore names Niman as his primary/peak form (100) while keeping legendary proficiency across every other form. This parallels Exar Kun (Era 04/05), the other rare true Niman master; Kun remains the singular apex at Niman 110.
> - **Mandalore the Ultimate's** death passes the Mask to Canderous Ordo, who becomes **Mandalore the Preserver** (Era 07/08).
> - **Bastila Shan** rises at the very end of this era; her full legendary-battle-meditation sheet (Battle Meditation 100) is in **Era 07**, where she captures and redeems Revan.
> - **Demagol/Pulsipher** and the **Muur Talisman** tie back to Era 02's Karness Muur — the talisman's Rakghoul plague resurfaces here.

> **CHANGE-NOTES (characters who fundamentally shift):**
> - **Revan — multi-sheet (Revanchist → Darth Revan → redeemed "Prodigal Knight").** This Era 06 sheet is the **Jedi Revanchist** — apex duelist-general, violet blade, legendary across dueling/leadership/Intelligence/Force, with adaptability across all seven forms. After Malachor he falls and becomes **Darth Revan** (crimson blade, Force lightning, life-drain, the Star Forge) — a *lateral* shift where **ForceOutput rises** (dark techniques) but his already-legendary control/strategy stay comparable. Then he's mind-wiped and rebuilt as a **redeemed Jedi**. All Dark Lord and redeemed sheets are in **Era 07**.
> - **Malak — multi-sheet (Alek → Darth Malak).** Jedi "Squint" is a powerful frontline warrior (Elite, FOut 86). As **Darth Malak** he loses his jaw, grows crueler, betrays Revan, and rises to Dark Lord — amplified dark power (FOut climbs into the 90s with Force lightning) and savagery. Dark Lord sheet in **Era 07**.
> - **Meetra Surik — multi-sheet (wartime Jedi → Force-severed Exile → reawakened Master).** Here she's a war-tempered Jedi General. At Malachor she **severs her own Force connection** (effectively non-Force-sensitive — all Force stats/abilities ~0 — for ~9 years). In **Era 08** she reawakens and rebuilds into a true Master who dismantles the Sith Triumvirate; that reawakened sheet belongs there.
> - **Canderous Ordo** (bench) → becomes **Mandalore the Preserver** later; a role shift rather than a power shift, so a single evolving profile suffices.
