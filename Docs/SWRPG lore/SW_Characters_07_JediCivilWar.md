# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 07: The Jedi Civil War (≈3,959–3,956 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Jedi Civil War** — the war waged by the Sith Empire of Darth Revan and Darth Malak against the Republic and Jedi Order, fueled by the ancient Rakatan **Star Forge**. This is the heart of *Knights of the Old Republic*: Revan's fall, betrayal, mind-wipe, redemption, and the destruction of the Star Forge at the Battle of Rakata Prime (3,956 BBY). Drawn from *Knights of the Old Republic* (BioWare) and supporting Legends sources.

This doc delivers the **Dark Lord sheets promised from Era 06** — Darth Revan, the redeemed "Prodigal Knight" Revan, and Darth Malak — plus Bastila Shan's legendary battle meditation and the KOTOR companions.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **Intelligence** — **Revan** (in every incarnation) is the era's legendary strategic mind (100). The assassin droid **HK-47** scores very high for his lethal cunning.
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude (distinct from ForceControl, which is finesse). Watch Revan's and Malak's dark sheets — Output rises with Force lightning and life-drain.

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every stat block (this sheet predated the v3 stats).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** all seven classic forms are codified by the KotOR era. Each duelist carries numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-saber fighters show *(none)* with a melee/ranged rating noted.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** each registry ability at base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`), with notable sub-ability proficiency percentages 0–100 in parentheses (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (Force connection-forging, life-drain in battle) flagged where they exceed a clean registry mapping.

### Era Notes on Scaling
The war-tempered Old Republic standard continues. **Revan** (in any incarnation) is one of the true apex beings of Legends — legendary across dueling, leadership, Intelligence, and Force breadth — and **Darth Malak** is a legendary Dark Lord in his own right. **Bastila Shan** is the era's example of a *non-combat legendary specialist*: her battle meditation could decide a galactic war by thought alone, even while her dueling is merely solid.

### Revan's primary form (Niman) — carried from Era 06
Per Drew Karpyshyn's stated intent, **Revan's primary form is Niman (Form VI)** — the adaptable, Force-integrating generalist form that he, like Exar Kun, raised to true mastery. This carries across all his sheets. His **dark** turn famously added the aggression of **Juyo** (the unpredictable ferocity with which he took Malak's jaw) and full **Jar'Kai** dual-wielding; his **redeemed** self disciplines that aggression back down while keeping Niman as his core.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Revan (III) — Darth Revan, Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Sheet snapshot:** Dark Lord prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, master of the Star Forge
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith Empire · Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** Fell ~3,959 BBY; betrayed by Malak and captured/mind-wiped ~3,957 BBY
- **Summary:** After Malachor V, Revan and Malak tracked a hidden Sith influence into the Unknown Regions, found the reconstituted Sith Empire, and were turned by the Sith Emperor (Vitiate). Breaking free of the Emperor's control, Revan became **Darth Revan**, took Malak as apprentice, and seized the ancient Rakatan **Star Forge** to build a vast fleet and wage the Jedi Civil War. He wielded a crimson blade and added Force lightning and life-drain to his already peerless arsenal. His fall was a *lateral* transformation: he was already legendary as the Revanchist, so Darth Revan represents the same apex power turned to dark techniques and ruthless application — until Malak's treachery shattered his command and left him for the Jedi to capture.

## Revan (IV) — the Prodigal Knight (redeemed Jedi)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Redeemed prime — amnesiac-rebuilt Jedi who destroys his own Sith Empire
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order / Republic · Jedi Knight (reborn), effectively Master-tier
- **Lifespan:** Mind-wiped ~3,957 BBY; redeemed and victorious at Rakata Prime 3,956 BBY; later departs for the Unknown Regions ~3,954 BBY
- **Summary:** Captured by Bastila Shan's strike team and saved by her when his ship was destroyed, the broken Darth Revan had his memory wiped by the Jedi Council and was rebuilt with a new identity, placed under Bastila's care in hopes he would subconsciously lead them to the Star Forge. Retrained as a Jedi on Dantooine, he rediscovered fragments of his past, fell in love with Bastila, and — confronted with the truth of who he was — chose the light. He gathered the *Ebon Hawk* companions, found the Star Forge, redeemed the dark-fallen Bastila, killed Darth Malak, and destroyed the Star Forge. He wielded blue and later green blades, embodying a hard-won balance of his dual nature.

## Malak (III) — Darth Malak, Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Sheet snapshot:** Dark Lord prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, master of the Star Forge
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith Empire · Sith apprentice → Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** Fell with Revan ~3,959 BBY; seized the Dark Lordship ~3,957 BBY; killed by Revan at the Star Forge 3,956 BBY
- **Summary:** Fallen alongside Revan, Alek became **Darth Malak**, Revan's apprentice. After losing his jaw in a duel (replaced with a metal prosthesis and vocabulator that gave him his iconic mechanical voice), he grew colder and crueler. When a Jedi strike team boarded Revan's flagship, Malak fired on the ship — apparently killing his master and claiming the Dark Lordship. As Dark Lord he waged the Civil War with savage efficiency, slaughtering the Jedi and, in his final desperation, **draining captive Jedi of their life through the Force** to replenish his power against the redeemed Revan — who killed him aboard the Star Forge.

## Bastila Shan
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Jedi Sentinel, battle-meditation prodigy
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order · Padawan → Knight → Master (Jedi Sentinel)
- **Lifespan:** Born ~3,979 BBY on Talravin; pivotal in the Jedi Civil War (3,959–3,956 BBY); survived the subsequent purge; later a Master and ancestor of the Shan line
- **Summary:** A Jedi Sentinel prodigy whose mastery — at a remarkably young age — of the rare art of **battle meditation** made her the single most strategically valuable Jedi in the war: able to bolster an entire fleet's morale and coordination while shattering the enemy's will, deciding battles by thought alone. The sole survivor of the strike team that captured Darth Revan, she saved his life and formed a deep Force bond with him, then guided the mind-wiped Revan toward the Star Forge. She fell in love with him; was captured and tortured to the dark side by Malak; was redeemed by Revan; and turned the Battle of Rakata Prime with her battle meditation. She married Revan, bore Vaner Shan, and became the **ancestor of Satele and Theron Shan** — and of the player-character bloodline in your campaign. Her dueling (a yellow double-bladed saber) is solid but secondary; her war-winning gift is the Force.

- **Signature Ability:** **Battle meditation (prodigy-level)** — among the most powerful battle-meditation users in history; could turn the tide of a galactic fleet engagement by thought alone, even as a young Padawan. Considered *the* key to winning the war.
- **Notable Feats:** Captured Darth Revan and saved his life, forming a lasting Force bond; resisted then succumbed to and was redeemed from the dark side; turned the Battle of Rakata Prime with her battle meditation; survived the post-war purge; matriarch of the Shan bloodline.
- **Alignment:** Light side (briefly fell to the dark under Malak's torture — see note).
- **Tier:** **Legendary** (battle meditation); Veteran-tier duelist.

Redeemed by Revan at the Star Forge. No separate sheet needed; treat the dark variant as identical stats with dark-side alignment and her Battle Meditation 100 aiding the Sith.
