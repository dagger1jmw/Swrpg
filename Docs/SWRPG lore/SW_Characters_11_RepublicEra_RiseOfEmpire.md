# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 11: The Republic Era & the Rise of the Empire (≈990–22 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the long Republic peace and the **prequel era** — the thousand-year span after Ruusan in which the Sith worked in secret under the Rule of Two while the Republic slowly decayed, culminating in the Invasion of Naboo (32 BBY) and the eve of the Clone Wars (22 BBY). This is the era of **Darth Plagueis and Darth Sidious**, the Naboo crisis and **Darth Maul**, the prequel **Jedi Council** (Yoda, Mace Windu, Qui-Gon Jinn, young Obi-Wan), and **Count Dooku's** turn toward the dark. Drawn from the *Darth Plagueis* novel (Luceno), *The Phantom Menace*, *Attack of the Clones*, the *Jedi Apprentice/Quest* material, and supporting Legends sources.

> **Continuity note (Legends, not Disney canon):** This doc follows **core Legends continuity**. Most importantly, **Darth Maul dies at the Battle of Naboo (32 BBY)** — killed by Obi-Wan Kenobi. The Clone Wars-era resurrection of Maul, Savage Opress, and the Maul/Mandalore/Satine arc are **Disney-canon (or late-EU) developments not part of core Legends** and are excluded here. The Clone Wars proper (Anakin's Knighthood, Grievous, Ventress, etc.) belong to **Era 12**.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **Intelligence** — the two Sith masterminds top the era: **Sidious** (the supreme manipulator of all Legends) and **Plagueis** (sorcerer-scientist-financier), both 100. **Maul** is the per-stat counterpoint — legendary dueling, Intelligence 64 ("bred to kill, not to rule").
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude. **Sidious's Force lightning** is the era's standout (ability 112).

### Era Notes on Scaling — **prequel Jedi skew LOWER**
A thousand years of peace **softened the Jedi Order**. By the prequel era most Jedi had never fought a war or dueled a Sith. The *individual greats* remain formidable — **Yoda** is the greatest of his time; **Mace Windu**, **Dooku**, and **Qui-Gon** are Master-to-legendary — but the rank-and-file Council Jedi sit in the **78–86 Force range** rather than the 85–95 of the war-tempered Old Republic. The Sith, concentrating power through the Rule of Two, field a few extremely dangerous individuals: **Sidious** is apex-adjacent, **Plagueis** a legendary sorcerer-scientist.

### Apex-hierarchy placement (per Era 09 APEX CEILING)
- **Chancellor-era Sidious** is **apex-adjacent** (ceiling ~112) — a peer of Bane at the **top of standard legendary**, with his full power still hidden. His **Emperor / Dark Empire** self (Era 13 and later) rises into the **Tier A apex cluster**; calibrated there alongside near-potential Anakin and Vader.
- No being in this era approaches peak Luke (~138) or Vitiate (~134).

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every block.
- **Form ratings (game stats):** the seven classic forms are fully codified and named here. Numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-saber fighters show *(none)* with a combat rating.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (midi-chlorian manipulation, shatterpoint, living-Force/spirit transcendence, the Grand Plan) flagged separately.

# THE SITH (RULE OF TWO)

> **Note — the keepers of the Sith legacy (post-Gravid):** Generations earlier, the mad Darth Gravid had tried to destroy the accumulated Sith knowledge, burning holocrons and artifacts before he was stopped — but much was lost forever. What *survived* passed down the Bane line to **Tenebrous → Plagueis → Sidious**, who among them held **nearly all the remaining Sith knowledge in the galaxy**. All three are accordingly **masters of Sith sorcery** with legendary ForceKnowledge — the living archive of the tradition Bane founded.

## Darth Plagueis (Hego Damask)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, master of midi-chlorian manipulation
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Muun · Sith (Rule of Two) · Dark Lord of the Sith; publicly Magister Hego Damask II of Damask Holdings / the InterGalactic Banking Clan
- **Lifespan:** Born ~120 BBY on Mygeeto; killed his master Tenebrous (67 BBY); murdered by Sidious ~32 BBY
- **Summary:** "Darth Plagueis the Wise" — a Muun Dark Lord and heir to Bane's lineage, trained by the Bith Sith Lord **Darth Tenebrous** (whom he murdered to claim the mantle). Obsessed with conquering death, he became the galaxy's foremost master of **midi-chlorian manipulation** — able to influence the midi-chlorians to create and sustain life, and to keep those he chose from dying. Operating publicly as the financier **Hego Damask**, he corroded the Republic through economics and politics while searching for immortality. He took the young Palpatine as his apprentice — and his meddling with life and death may have provoked the Force into creating the Chosen One (Anakin). Sidious murdered him in his sleep.

## Darth Sidious (Sheev Palpatine) — Senator/Chancellor-era
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, hidden architect of the Republic's fall (pre-Empire)
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith (Rule of Two) · Dark Lord of the Sith; publicly Senator → Supreme Chancellor of the Republic
- **Lifespan:** Born ~82 BBY on Naboo; apprenticed to Plagueis; killed Plagueis ~32 BBY; Chancellor from 32 BBY; declares the Empire 19 BBY (Era 13)
- **Summary:** The Sith master who would topple the thousand-year Republic. Born Sheev Palpatine of Naboo, seduced and trained by Plagueis, he murdered him to claim the Dark Lordship and executed the **Grand Plan** with patient genius: as Senator then Supreme Chancellor he played every faction against the others, engineering the Naboo crisis, the Separatist movement, and the Clone Wars — all to make himself Emperor. He directed his apprentices **Maul** then **Tyranus (Dooku)** as blades while he worked in plain sight. Here he is at the height of his hidden powers; his open, disfigured **Emperor** incarnation is Era 13. A legendary sorcerer (Force lightning, sorcery, foresight) and the supreme manipulator of all Legends.

- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Yoda's species (unnamed) · Jedi Order · Grand Master, senior member of the High Council
- **Lifespan:** ~900 years old by this era (born ~896 BBY); leads the Order through the prequel era and Clone Wars; dies in exile 4 ABY (Era 13)
- **Summary:** The diminutive, ancient Grand Master — the Order's wisest and most powerful member, with nearly nine centuries of experience. Trained by N'Kata Del Gormo, he trained generations of Jedi (Dooku among them). Despite his size and age he was the **greatest lightsaber duelist of his time**, the only Jedi to surpass Mace Windu and Dooku, fighting with a green-bladed shoto and astonishing Force-driven acrobatics. His true mastery is the depth of his Force connection and wisdom. He failed, as all the Jedi did, to perceive the Sith's return in time.

Wielding a unique amethyst blade, he was one of the few Jedi who could face a Sith Lord and win — and came closest of any Jedi to defeating Sidious in single combat.

- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order · Master (declined a Council seat)
- **Lifespan:** Active through the late prequel era; killed by Darth Maul at the Battle of Naboo (32 BBY)

He dueled **Darth Maul** on Naboo and was killed — the first sign in a thousand years that the Sith had returned. He later became one of the first Jedi to achieve consciousness after death as a Force spirit, a discovery he passed to Yoda and Obi-Wan.

## Obi-Wan Kenobi (young — Padawan/new Knight)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Early prime — Qui-Gon's Padawan → newly knighted Jedi (the Sith-slayer of Naboo)
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order · Padawan → Jedi Knight
- **Lifespan:** Born 57 BBY on Stewjon; Padawan under Qui-Gon; knighted after Naboo (32 BBY); his Clone Wars prime is Era 12, his Ben Kenobi self Era 13

This made him the **first Jedi in ~1,000 years to defeat a Sith Lord** — but a near-run thing against a superior duelist. He fought with **Form IV (Ataru)** here; only *after* the duel did he abandon it — blaming its poor defense for Qui-Gon's death — and begin developing the **Form III (Soresu)** mastery that would define him.

## Count Dooku (Jedi Master, pre-fall)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — revered Jedi Master and master swordsman, on the cusp of leaving the Order
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (Serennian noble) · Jedi Order · Master, former Council member; heir to the Count of Serenno
- **Lifespan:** Born ~102 BBY; nearly 70 years a Jedi; left the Order ~32–30 BBY after Qui-Gon's death; becomes Darth Tyranus (Era 12)
- **Summary:** One of the most respected Masters of the age — Yoda's former Padawan, Qui-Gon's master, a renowned Temple instructor. As a swordsman he was **surpassed only by Yoda and equaled only by Mace Windu**, the supreme master of **Form II (Makashi)**. Decades of disillusionment with the Republic's corruption and the Jedi's complacency — crystallized by Galidraan and Qui-Gon's death — drove him to leave the Order, claim Serenno, and fall under Sidious's influence. This is his **Jedi-Master prime**; **Darth Tyranus** (and the Clone Wars) belong to **Era 12**.
