# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 16: Legacy of the Force & the Second Galactic Civil War (≈40 ABY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Second Galactic Civil War** — the tragedy in which **Jacen Solo**, the war-hero of the Yuuzhan Vong conflict (Era 15), is manipulated by the Dark Lady **Lumiya** into falling to the dark side and becoming **Darth Caedus**, plunging the galaxy into civil war and forcing his own family to destroy him. It is the era of **Mara Jade's death**, **Luke Skywalker near his lifetime peak**, and **Jaina Solo** — the Sword of the Jedi — fulfilling her destiny by killing her twin brother. Drawn from the *Legacy of the Force* novel series and supporting Legends sources.

> **Continuity note (Legends):** Core Legends — Legends-only. The galaxy is governed by the **Galactic Alliance**; the conflict pits it against a **Confederation** of secessionist worlds, with the **Imperial Remnant** (under Pellaeon, then Jagged Fel) and the **Mandalorians** (under Mandalore Boba Fett) as major players. The **Lost Tribe of the Sith / Abeloth** (Fate of the Jedi, ~43 ABY, Era 17) and **Darth Krayt's One Sith / Cade Skywalker** (Legacy era, ~130 ABY, Era 18) lie ahead.

Stats represent each character **at their prime for this era**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl (finesse) · ForceOutput (raw power).

### Era Notes on Scaling — ★ LUKE NEAR APEX (true peak is Era 17) ★
Luke Skywalker is **near the height of his power** here — but, importantly, **this is NOT yet his absolute apex.** Per the APEX CEILING, his lifetime peak (**~138, strongest realized mortal, second only to Abeloth**) is the **Fate of the Jedi** Luke who battles **Abeloth** (Era 17, ~43 ABY). The Luke of *this* era — who confronts **Darth Caedus** — is a near-apex Luke (Output ~134) who, in the actual confrontation, was **holding back and trying to end the fight fast** (and whose foresight was clouded after he killed Lumiya in grief). That is why **Caedus landed real hits on him and held the line for a time** — not because they were equals, but because this was not a full-power, all-out Luke. The narrative still has Luke clearly *more powerful* than Caedus; Jaina, not Luke, deals the killing blow because Luke's clouded vision steered the duty to the Sword of the Jedi.

> **Apex hierarchy (per Era 09 APEX CEILING):** **Abeloth ≈ 185 — Transcendent, off the scale (Era 17), in a tier of her own** · then the mortal apex: **peak Luke ≈ 138 (Era 17)** · reborn-Emperor ≈ 135 (Era 14) / Vitiate ≈ 134 / Krayt ≈ 132 (Era 18) / living-Emperor ≈ 130 · then Knightfall Anakin ≈ 120, Bane ≈ 114. **Era 16 Luke ≈ 134 (near-apex) and Caedus ≈ 120 (near-apex Sith) both sit below the true peak Luke of Era 17 — and far below Abeloth.**

### Multi-sheet payoff (the big one)
- **Jacen Solo → Darth Caedus.** The hero of Era 15 falls. This is his **Sith sheet** — darker, near-apex, the inverse of the enlightened Oneness he achieved in the Vong War. A full transformation, like Anakin → Vader (but his raw power *rose* with the fall).

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric forms & abilities
- **Form ratings (game stats):** numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-Force fighters show a combat rating. (Intelligence and ForceOutput were already in use.)
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (flow-walking, the lightwhip, etc.) flagged separately.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Luke Skywalker (near-apex — Legacy era)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Near-apex prime — Grand Master of the Jedi Order, just short of his absolute peak
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · New Jedi Order · Grand Master
- **Lifespan:** Born 19 BBY; near his lifetime peak here (~40 ABY); reaches his absolute apex in Fate of the Jedi (~43 ABY, Era 17)
- **Summary:** Luke at the near-summit of his lifelong ascent — from undertrained farmboy (Era 13) to founder (Era 14) to wartime Grand Master (Era 15) to, now, the most powerful Jedi of his age and one of the most powerful in galactic history. His mastery is effectively total — raw **Output** and refined **Control** unmatched by any living being and, across all Legends, second only to Abeloth. The war strikes him personally: his nephew Jacen falls and **murders Mara**, Luke's wife. In grief Luke kills the Dark Lady **Lumiya** (whom he wrongly blamed) — an act that **clouded his vision of the future**, which is why he did not face Caedus to the end himself and entrusted that duty to Jaina. **Crucially, this is not yet his full power:** the Luke who soon battles **Abeloth** (Era 17) is stronger still. Against Caedus he was a near-apex Luke trying to end the fight fast, not an all-out one — which is how Caedus held the line and landed hits, even though Luke was the clearly superior fighter.

In the final confrontation she defeated and killed **Darth Caedus** — who, in death, returned to the light as Jacen and used his last thought to save his daughter Allana. ## Darth Caedus (Jacen Solo, fallen)
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, dictator of the Galactic Alliance; near-apex
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Sith (Lumiya's apprentice, then Dark Lord) · Dark Lord of the Sith; Galactic Alliance Chief of State (dictator)
- **Lifespan:** Born 9 ABY; falls to the dark side ~40 ABY; killed by Jaina Solo the same year
- **Summary:** The fall of the New Jedi Order's greatest young hero. Manipulated by the Dark Lady **Lumiya** — who convinced him only a Sith could impose the peace the galaxy needed, and that he must sacrifice what he loved most — Jacen Solo descended into darkness, **murdered his aunt Mara Jade**, and was reborn as **Darth Caedus**. He seized dictatorial control of the Galactic Alliance, tortured his apprentice-cousin **Ben**, took the fallen **Tahiri Veila** as his Sith apprentice, and waged brutal war on his own family. As Jacen he was already **second only to Luke among the Jedi as a swordsman** (Era 15); as Caedus that elite dueling fused with a dark-amplified command of the Force said to **surpass even his grandfather Darth Vader's** — including **flow-walking** (perceiving and walking through past/future), battle meditation, immense telekinesis, and Force lightning. He pressed even Luke (who was holding back and trying to end the fight fast), landing real hits before being killed by his twin **Jaina** — returning to the light as Jacen in his final moment to save his daughter Allana. A genuinely tragic near-apex Sith.
