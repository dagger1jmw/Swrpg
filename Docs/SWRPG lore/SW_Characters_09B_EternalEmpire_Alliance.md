# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 09-B: The Eternal Empire, the Alliance & the Later SWTOR Era (≈3,637–3,620 BBY and beyond)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Zakuul / Eternal Empire** arc and everything after it in *Star Wars: The Old Republic* — *Knights of the Fallen Empire* (KotFE), *Knights of the Eternal Throne* (KotET), and the post-throne expansions (*Iokath*, *Ossus/Jedi Under Siege*, *Onslaught*, *Legacy of the Sith*, and the ongoing story). It covers the **Eternal Empire royal family**, the **Eternal Alliance** leadership and companions, and the major figures of the later conflicts.

### IMPORTANT CONTINUITY NOTE
All of this content was released **after April 2014**, when Disney rebranded the Expanded Universe as "Legends." By Lucasfilm's strict definition it is therefore **not technically part of "Legends"** the way KOTOR and vanilla SWTOR are. However, it is one continuous SWTOR story with no Disney-canon overlap, so this project treats it as **Legends-adjacent / the continuation of the Old Republic Legends story.** Flagging it so the status is explicit.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** See the **APEX CEILING** in Era 09 for the 126–150 sub-bands. |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude. This era's showcase is **Vaylin**, whose raw telekinetic might (FOut ~108) lets her tear apart fleets — yet her shattered psyche keeps her Intelligence low (the per-stat philosophy again: legendary in raw power, broken elsewhere).
- **Intelligence** — the schemer-strategists top it: the superintelligence **SCORPIO** (95), spymasters **Lana / Theron** (86), and the technologist rulers **Acina/Vowrawn** (86).

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every block (this sheet predated the v3 stats).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** all seven classic forms are codified. Numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-saber fighters show *(none)* with a melee/ranged rating noted.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.

### Valkorion = Vitiate (recalibrated — see Era 09 APEX CEILING)
**Valkorion is the same entity as Vitiate/Tenebrae** — apex sheet in Era 09, **recalibrated below peak Luke**: ceiling ~134 (FKnow 134 / WIL 135 / FCtrl 130 / FOut 130), with his galactic life-drain as a specialist ability rated ~145. This doc covers the *Valkorion identity and Eternal Empire context* and his **final destruction**; it does not re-stat him. His original Zakuulan host body (the man Valkorion) was a mere mortal warrior whose mind Vitiate suppressed.

Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## Valkorion — the Immortal Emperor (pointer)
- **Sheet snapshot:** *Pointer entry* — same being as **Vitiate/Tenebrae**; recalibrated apex stats in **Era 09**
- **Summary:** When the Force entity Vitiate needed a new identity, he possessed a Zakuulan warrior named **Valkorion**, suppressing the man's mind entirely and using his body to secretly build and rule the **Eternal Empire** for centuries as the "Immortal Emperor" — his true master project, with the Sith Empire a mere "side project." After consuming Ziost (Era 09), he ruled Zakuul openly until his body was destroyed in 3,636 BBY by Arcann and the Outlander; his spirit then lodged in the Outlander's mind. He made a final bid to possess the Commander and was **destroyed permanently** by the Commander, Senya, and Arcann using Dramath's holocron — ending his ~1,500-year existence.
- **Stats:** Use the **recalibrated Vitiate apex sheet (Era 09)** — FKnow 134 / WIL 135 / FCtrl 130 / FOut 130 / FSense 126, with the galactic life-drain ability at 145. **Apex dark-side *specialist*, below peak Luke (~138).** The mortal host "Valkorion" before possession was an ordinary elite warrior (no apex stats).
- **Tier:** **LEGENDARY — APEX (Tier A, ceiling ~134).** The most powerful dark-side being in Legends — but the apex *specialist*, not the apex being (see Era 09 APEX CEILING).
- **Cross-era note:** Final incarnation and final death. Origin in Era 03; apex in Era 09; ends here.

- **Lifespan:** Son of Valkorion and Senya Tirall; seized the throne 3,636 BBY; ruled 5 years; defeated, redeemed, and joined the Alliance

He ruled as a harsh, paranoid Emperor for five years until the Outlander's Alliance defeated him; healed by his mother on Voss, he renounced his past, sought redemption, and returned to **aid the Alliance** against his sister Vaylin.

## Vaylin
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Empress of the Eternal Empire, the most raw-powerful of Valkorion's children
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (Force-sensitive) · Eternal Empire · princess → Empress of Zakuul
- **Lifespan:** Daughter of Valkorion and Senya; seized the throne after Arcann; killed by the Outlander/Commander ~3,632 BBY
- **Summary:** The youngest child of Senya and the entity in Valkorion, Vaylin displayed **terrifying, prodigious Force power from early childhood** — so much that Valkorion, fearing she could one day challenge even him, locked her away on Nathema and subjected her to brutal mental conditioning that shattered her empathy and left her a sadistic, unstable force of destruction. Freed of her conditioning, she became arguably the **single most raw-powerful Force-user of the era** — able to telekinetically rip apart structures and fleets — and seized the Eternal Throne. Her instability was her only weakness; the Commander finally killed her.

## The Alliance Commander / the Outlander (pointer)
- **Sheet snapshot:** *Pointer entry* — the player-protagonist of KotFE/KotET; scales to their origin class
- **Summary:** The **Outlander** — a famed champion (originally one of the eight class protagonists; see Era 09) who opposed Valkorion at Yavin 4 and Ziost — was captured by Arcann, blamed for Valkorion's death, and frozen in carbonite for five years. Freed by Lana Beniko, they founded and led the **Eternal Alliance** from Odessen, defeated Arcann and Vaylin, seized the Eternal Throne, destroyed Valkorion's spirit, and became the most powerful single figure in the galaxy (commanding the Eternal Fleet).
- **Stats:** Use the relevant **Era 09 "Eight" class sheet** — whichever class the Outlander is. Treat as **legendary-adjacent** at the height of their power (a champion who personally fought Valkorion, Arcann, and Vaylin and held Valkorion's spirit in their mind).
- **Tier:** **Legendary**-adjacent (varies by class).
- **Cross-era note:** Carries the Commander mantle through Onslaught and Legacy of the Sith.
