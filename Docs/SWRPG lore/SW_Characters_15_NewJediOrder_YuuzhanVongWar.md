# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 15: The New Jedi Order & the Yuuzhan Vong War (≈25–29 ABY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **Yuuzhan Vong War** — the galaxy's most devastating conflict, in which extragalactic invaders from beyond known space nearly exterminated all life in the galaxy and reduced the New Republic to the brink of collapse before the Galactic Alliance and the New Jedi Order turned the tide. It is the defining trial of Luke Skywalker's New Order, the war that **kills Chewbacca and Anakin Solo**, forges **Jaina** into the Sword of the Jedi, and sets **Jacen Solo** on the philosophical path that will eventually make him Darth Caedus (Era 16). Drawn from *The New Jedi Order* novel series (*Vector Prime* through *The Unifying Force*), and supporting Legends sources.

> **Continuity note (Legends):** Core Legends — this entire era is Legends-only (no Disney-canon equivalent). The New Republic becomes the **Galactic Federation of Free Alliances (Galactic Alliance)** mid-war. The war ends with the living planet **Zonama Sekot** brokering peace; surviving Vong are relocated under its care.

Stats represent each character **at their prime for this era**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### ★ ERA MECHANIC — THE YUUZHAN VONG ARE INVISIBLE TO THE FORCE ★
The single defining feature of this era. The **Yuuzhan Vong exist outside the Force** — they cast no presence in it and cannot be perceived by it. This is **fundamentally different from merely being non-Force-sensitive**:
- A non-Force-sensitive being (Han Solo, a stormtrooper) still *exists in the Force* — a Jedi can sense them, read their emotions, mind-trick them, and hurl them telekinetically.
- A **Yuuzhan Vong cannot be sensed, read, mind-tricked, or (reliably) touched by telekinesis at all.** To a Jedi's Force-senses they are a *hole in the universe*. Precognition fails against them; danger-sense doesn't trigger; mind tricks slide off.

**Combat implications:** Jedi fighting the Vong must rely on **physical skill, reflexes, and lightsaber deflection** rather than Force perception and telekinetic control — a massive equalizer that cost many Jedi their lives early in the war. Their bio-weapons compound this: **amphistaffs** (resist lightsabers), **vonduun crab armor** (lightsaber-resistant), **thud/razor bugs**, and **dovin basals** (swallow energy, pin a Jedi's telekinesis). A few Jedi (notably **Jacen Solo**, via Vergere's *unifying Force*) learned to perceive the Vong *indirectly* — sensing the Force flowing *around* the void they create.

**Notation:** Yuuzhan Vong Force rows are marked **"✗ Force-invisible"** — not the "—" used for ordinary non-sensitives. The lone exception is **Onimi**, the only Force-sensitive Yuuzhan Vong in existence.

### Apex-ladder application (per Era 09 APEX CEILING)
- **Luke (Grand Master)** continues ascending — **Output 125 / Control 116**, near-apex — but still below his reserved apex (**~138, strongest realized mortal, second only to Abeloth**), which peaks in **Era 17 (Fate of the Jedi)**. No being in this era exceeds him.
- The Vong's Force-invisibility flattens the usual Jedi advantage, so this era is graded partly on **martial skill and will** rather than raw Force power.

## Jacen Solo (NJO)

- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · New Jedi Order · Jedi Knight (Skywalker–Solo line)
- **Lifespan:** Born 9 ABY; central hero of the war; becomes **Darth Caedus** in 40 ABY (Era 16)
- **Summary:** The elder Solo twin — a thoughtful, philosophical young Jedi who entered the war agonizing over the morality of violence. Captured by the Yuuzhan Vong after the Myrkr mission (where his brother Anakin died), he endured months in the **Embrace of Pain** and the ambiguous tutelage of **Vergere**, who taught him that the Force is a *single unifying whole* — and that the Vong's "absence" was a matter of perception. Transformed, Jacen emerged extraordinarily powerful and learned to perceive the Vong indirectly. At the war's climax he achieved **Oneness with the Force**, killed the mastermind **Onimi**, and helped end the war. One of the strongest and most enlightened Jedi of the age — which makes his later fall all the more tragic.
