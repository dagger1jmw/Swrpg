# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets
## Era 10: The New Sith Wars & the Rise of Darth Bane (≈2,000–990 BBY)

---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the **New Sith Wars** — the thousand-year twilight struggle that ended the ancient Sith Empires and nearly destroyed the Republic — and the **rise of Darth Bane**, whose Rule of Two reforged the Sith into the hidden, two-being order that would eventually topple the Republic a millennium later. Centered on the climactic Ruusan campaign (1,000 BBY). Drawn from the *Darth Bane* trilogy (*Path of Destruction*, *Rule of Two*, *Dynasty of Evil*) by Drew Karpyshyn, the *Jedi vs. Sith* comic, and supporting Legends sources.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale**.

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)
| Band | Range | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Novice / civilian | 10–40 | Untrained or non-combatant |
| Trained | 40–60 | Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior |
| Veteran / elite | 60–85 | Seasoned Knight, skilled commander |
| Master | 85–100 | Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users |
| **Legendary** | **100–150** | Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.** See the **APEX CEILING** in Era 09 for the 126–150 sub-bands. |

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)
Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)
**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.
- **Intelligence** — **Darth Bane** tops the era at 100: a strategic genius on Revan's level who conceived the thousand-year Rule-of-Two plan and manipulated an entire Sith order into self-destruction. **Zannah** (mind-sorcery) and the war-generals (Hoth, Farfalla) score high.
- **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude (distinct from ForceControl, finesse). Bane's orbalisk-amplified dark power is his standout.

### Where Bane sits in the apex hierarchy
Bane is **one of the greatest Sith in history** — but per the Era 09 APEX CEILING, he sits at the **top of standard legendary (ceiling ~114), just below the Tier A apex cluster** (Vitiate ~134, Krayt ~132, near-potential Anakin ~135). The true god-tier Sith (the planet-draining Vitiate; the near-immortal, all-Sith-uniting Krayt) edge above even the Sith'ari. His **orbalisk armor** is the force-multiplier that lets him punch at that ceiling.

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats + v3 stats
- **ForceOutput and Intelligence** added to every block (this sheet predated the v3 stats).
- **Form ratings (game stats):** all seven classic forms are in mature use. Numeric per-form ratings (0–150) → `lightsaberForms.*`. Non-saber/lightwhip fighters noted with a combat rating.
- **Force Abilities (game stats):** registry base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.*`) + sub-ability proficiency 0–100% (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
- **Unique techniques** (orbalisk symbiosis, the thought bomb, mind-attack sorcery, lightwhip) flagged separately.

### Schema
Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (named forms + numeric ratings) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

# THE SITH — DARTH BANE & HIS LINE

## Darth Bane
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, the Sith'ari, founder of the Rule of Two
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (born Dessel of Apatros) · Brotherhood of Darkness → his own reborn Sith Order · Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** Born ~1,026 BBY on Apatros; founded the Rule of Two at Ruusan (1,000 BBY); died ~980 BBY on Ambria, killed by his apprentice Zannah
- **Summary:** A miner from Apatros who killed a man with the Force, fled into the Sith army, and rose through the Brotherhood of Darkness — only to conclude that the Brotherhood's "many Sith sharing power" had made the order weak. Studying Darth Revan's holocron on Lehon, he resolved to destroy the Brotherhood and rebuild the Sith. He manipulated Lord Kaan into detonating the **thought bomb** at Ruusan (concealing that it would kill every Force-user present), wiping out both the Brotherhood and the bulk of the Jedi Army of Light — and survived as the lone Sith. He founded the **Rule of Two** (one Master, one apprentice), bonded with the parasitic **orbalisk armor** that vastly amplified his strength and dark power, took **Zannah** as apprentice, and engineered the Sith's thousand-year shadow war that would culminate in Palpatine. Recognized as the **Sith'ari** — the prophesied perfect being who would destroy and remake the Sith. He died as the Rule of Two intended: killed by his own apprentice.

- **Lightsaber Forms / Bladework:** A **complete master of all seven classic forms**, studied in depth at the Korriban academy — but he favors **Form V (Djem So/Shien)**, channeling his immense physical power and the orbalisks' enhancement into overwhelming, momentum-crushing offense. Cold, methodical brutality, fusing blade-work with Force lightning, telekinetic strikes, and raw dark-side power. His orbalisk armor renders him nearly impervious to lightsabers, letting him trade blows no normal duelist could survive — which (with a collapsing temple) is how he beat the more technically gifted Blademaster Kas'im.
  **Form ratings (game stats):** Shien/Djem So 104 (primary) · Juyo 94 · Soresu 92 · Shii-Cho 90 · Ataru 90 · Makashi 88 · Niman 88

- **Unique techniques:** **Orbalisk armor symbiosis** *(parasitic living armor — near-invulnerability to lightsabers, amplified strength, regeneration, and dark-side power)*
- **Signature Ability:** **The Rule of Two & the Sith'ari** — reforged the entire Sith tradition into a hidden master-and-apprentice line designed to grow ever stronger across generations; the prophesied perfect Sith, augmented by orbalisk armor.
- **Notable Feats:** Manipulated the Brotherhood into self-annihilation via the thought bomb at Ruusan; sole Sith survivor; founded the Rule of Two; bonded with orbalisk armor; killed Lord Valenthyne Farfalla and a Jedi strike team on Tython; established the lineage that produced Sidious.
- **Alignment:** Dark side.
- **Tier:** **Legendary** (dueling / will / sorcery / survivability) — the greatest Sith of his era, at the **top of standard legendary, just below the Vitiate/Krayt apex cluster** (Era 09 APEX CEILING).

## Darth Zannah
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lady of the Sith, Bane's apprentice and successor
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human (born Rain) · Bane's Sith Order · Sith apprentice → Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** A child at Ruusan (1,000 BBY); trained ~20 years; killed and succeeded Bane ~980 BBY on Ambria
- **Summary:** A young girl named Rain, orphaned at Ruusan, whom Bane took as his apprentice — sensing her capacity to channel grief into power. Over two decades he forged her into a fearsome Sith. Unusually, her genius lay not in dueling but in **Sith sorcery**: illusion, mind-domination, and using the Force against the mind — she could shatter an enemy's psyche or drive them mad. Patient and calculating, she ultimately challenged and killed Bane on Ambria to claim the mantle, taking **Darth Cognus** as her own apprentice.

- **Lightsaber Forms / Bladework:** Wields a **double-bladed saber** in a committed **Form III (Soresu)** defensive style — her small frame and modest strength made pure aggression a losing game, so she specialized in tireless, airtight defense instead. This was deliberate: Soresu let her **weather a stronger opponent's onslaught and conserve energy until she could bring her sorcery to bear** — exactly how she ultimately beat Bane on Ambria, turtling through his power attacks until she could unleash her dark-side sorcery on him. Her dueling is otherwise unremarkable; the defense is the whole point.

- **Signature Ability:** **Sith sorcery (mind-attack & illusion)** — a master of weaponizing the Force against the mind; far more dangerous through sorcery than through her saber. Her **Soresu defense is the delivery system**: outlast a stronger foe with airtight defense, then break them with sorcery (her winning strategy against Bane).
- **Notable Feats:** Survived Ruusan as a child; trained two decades under Bane; killed Jedi Masters Raskta Lsu and Sarro Xaj on Tython; drove the Jedi Darovit insane; killed and succeeded Darth Bane.
- **Alignment:** Dark side.
- **Tier:** **Legendary** (Sith sorcery / mind-attack); Veteran–elite duelist.

## Lord Skere Kaan
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Dark Lord of the Sith, leader of the Brotherhood of Darkness ("the Dark One")
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi (former) → Brotherhood of Darkness · Dark Lord of the Sith
- **Lifespan:** A Jedi who fell late in the New Sith Wars; led the Brotherhood for a decade; died at Ruusan (1,000 BBY) detonating his own thought bomb
- **Summary:** A charismatic Jedi Master who defected to the Sith and unified the warring Sith factions into the **Brotherhood of Darkness** — thousands of Sith who abandoned the single-Dark-Lord title for shared power (everyone a "Dark Lord"). A gifted orator nicknamed "the Dark One," he ruled the Sith for a decade of war against the Jedi. As Ruusan ground his forces down, he slid into paranoia and madness, and was manipulated by Bane into detonating the **thought bomb** — which annihilated him, his Brotherhood, and most of the Army of Light. His power-dispersed leadership was exactly what Bane's Rule of Two was created to replace.

## Lord Hoth
- **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — Jedi Lord, Supreme commander of the Army of Light
- **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Human · Jedi Order · Jedi Lord, General / Commander of the Republic Military
- **Lifespan:** Rallied the Army of Light (~1,010 BBY); died at Ruusan (1,000 BBY) in the thought-bomb sacrifice
- **Summary:** The greatest Jedi general of the New Sith Wars. Frustrated by the Republic's dispersed war effort, Hoth rallied the Jedi Lords into a single unified fighting force — the **Army of Light** — and became commander of the Republic Military. A brilliant, fiery battlefield leader of legendary temper, he drove the Brotherhood from world after world. Knowing Kaan would unleash the thought bomb, he led a hundred Jedi into the cave to force the confrontation anyway — sacrificing himself and them to end the war and begin the thousand-year peace.

*Era 10 of the Star Wars Legends character-sheet series. Stats are prime-version, galaxy-wide scale. **Now fully game-ready: ForceOutput + Intelligence on every block, numeric named-form ratings, and numeric Force Ability stats (base level + sub-ability proficiency %).** Bane sits at the top of standard legendary (ceiling ~114), just below the Vitiate/Krayt Tier A apex cluster; Kas'im the Blademaster carries the era's highest pure-technique form ratings (Jar'Kai 100), out-fencing even Bane.*

> **Cross-era appearance notes:**
> - **Darth Bane's** Rule of Two is the direct ancestor of every later Sith — Zannah → Cognus → … → Plagueis → Sidious → Maul/Tyranus/Vader (Eras 11–13). His holocron and teachings echo down the entire line.
> - **Darth Zannah → Darth Cognus** continue the Rule of Two into the long Republic peace.
> - The **thought bomb** ritual traces back to **Vitiate's Nathema ritual** (Era 03/09) via **Darth Revan's holocron** (Era 07) — a through-line if those artifacts surface in play.
> - **Tython** (the Je'daii homeworld, Era 01) reappears as the site of Bane and Zannah's battle against the Jedi strike team — and **Belia Darzu's** fortress.

> **CHANGE-NOTES (characters who fundamentally shift):**
> - **No multi-sheet splits needed this era.** Bane and Zannah each have a single dramatic arc; neither undergoes a power-transforming second incarnation the way Revan or Exar Kun did.
> - **Darth Bane placement — consistency fix.** His prior-session stats topped at WIL 110; he's now set with a ceiling ~114 (WIL 114 / FOut 112) plus **Intelligence 100** for his strategic genius, and explicitly positioned at the **top of standard legendary, just below the Tier A apex cluster** (Vitiate/Krayt). The Era 09 APEX CEILING reference is being adjusted to match (Bane was loosely listed in Tier A; he sits just beneath it). His orbalisk armor is the force-multiplier that lets him fight at that ceiling.
> - **Kas'im vs Bane** — a deliberate per-stat distinction: Kas'im is the **superior pure technician** (highest form ratings of the era, Jar'Kai 100), but Bane's vastly higher Force power, will, and orbalisk resilience won their duel. Captures the source material exactly.
> - **Lord Kaan** — his *prime* stats are high-Master, but by Ruusan he had descended into paranoid madness; depict late-Ruusan Kaan with badly impaired judgment.
