# Star Wars Legends — Character Sheets

## Era 02: Old Republic Founding \& First Conflicts (≈28,000–5,100 BBY)

\---

## ABOUT THIS DOCUMENT

Character reference for the long stretch between the Dawn of the Jedi and the Golden Age of the Sith — the founding of the Galactic Republic (25,053 BBY), the First Great Schism (24,500 BBY), and the Hundred-Year Darkness with its Dark Jedi Exiles who founded the first Sith Empire (Battle of Corbos, 6,900 BBY). It also reaches back to **King Adas** (\~28,000 BBY), the original Sith'ari.

Stats represent each character **at their prime**, on the **galaxy-wide Legends scale** (consistent across every era doc).

### Stat Scale (galaxy-wide, all of Legends)

|Band|Range|Meaning|
|-|-|-|
|Novice / civilian|10–40|Untrained or non-combatant|
|Trained|40–60|Competent Knight-equivalent, rank-and-file warrior|
|Veteran / elite|60–85|Seasoned Knight, skilled commander|
|Master|85–100|Master-tier; ceiling for nearly all Force users|
|**Legendary**|**100–150**|Mastery far beyond any normal Jedi or Sith. **Per-stat, not blanket.**|

### Stat tiers (matches game engine)

Initiate 0–20 · Padawan 21–40 · Knight 41–60 · Master 61–80 · Grandmaster 81–100 · Legendary 101–150 · Transcendent 151+

### Stats in use (v3 — six core, five Force)

**Core:** Strength · Agility · Endurance · Willpower · Charisma · **Intelligence**. **Force:** ForceSense · Meditation · ForceKnowledge · ForceControl · **ForceOutput**.

* **Intelligence** — this era's intellectual architects of the Sith tradition score very high; **Sorzus Syn tops the era** as the codifier of Sith alchemy, philosophy, and lore.
* **ForceOutput** = raw power/magnitude (distinct from ForceControl, which is finesse).

### NEW IN THIS PASS — game-ready numeric stats

Every combatant now carries **numeric stats**:

* **Bladework rating (game stats):** formal lightsaber forms did not exist this era. Each armed fighter gets a single **Armed-Combat / Bladework rating** (0–150) for skill with primitive sabers, Sith war-swords, axes, or melee. In the engine, enter in the foundational combat slot (`lightsaberForms.ShiiCho` as "base armed combat") or a custom `Bladework` entry. Non-combatants show *(none)*.
* **Force Abilities (game stats):** each registry ability at base level 0–150 (→ `forceAbilities.\*`), with notable sub-ability proficiency percentages 0–100 in parentheses (→ `forceAbilityProficiency`). Bands: Novice 0–25 · Developing 26–50 · Proficient 51–70 · Expert 71–90 · Mastered 91–100.
* Abilities sharing a name with a Force stat (Force Sense) use that stat value as the base level.
* **Unique techniques** (Teräs Käsi, morichro, spirit-binding) flagged separately — morichro maps to a Mastered Force Stasis specialization.

### Era Notes on Scaling

This era contains the **architects of the entire Sith tradition** — the twelve Exiles who invented Sith alchemy, sorcery, and the Sith Order itself. Several reach legendary tier in their specialties (Sorzus Syn, Karness Muur, XoXaan, Ajunta Pall). These were **Dark Jedi**, not the hyper-refined Sith of later millennia; their raw combat power is generally Master-tier while their *innovations* are legendary. King Adas is a near-immortal god-king with legendary physical/sorcery stats.

### Era Notes on Combat

Lightsabers existed in primitive form by the Hundred-Year Darkness, but **formal combat forms had not been codified.** Bladework entries describe combat method **plus a numeric Armed-Combat rating**. Arden Lyn is the one notable martial specialist (Teräs Käsi, unarmed).

### Schema

Each sheet: **Core Stats** (6) · **Force Stats** (5) · **Lightsaber Forms/Bladework** (method + numeric rating) · **Force Abilities (game stats)** (numeric) · **Unique techniques** (where any) · **Signature Ability** · **Notable Feats** · **Alignment**.

## King Adas — the original Sith'ari

* **Sheet snapshot:** Prime — King of Korriban, the first Sith'ari
* **Species / Affiliation / Rank:** Sith (species) · Kingdom of the Sith · King / Sith'ari ("Overlord")
* **Lifespan:** Reigned nearly 300 years; united Korriban \~28,000 BBY; died \~27,700 BBY repelling the Rakatan invasion
* **Summary:** A massive Sith in ebon warrior armor, raised as a chosen being. With his alchemically forged battle-axe he united Korriban's warring nations and ruled as the first Sith'ari — believed immortal. When the Rakatan Infinite Empire arrived and taught him holocron technology, he realized their intent and drove the invaders off, dying in the effort. His death shattered the Sith into millennia of civil war, and the Sith'ari prophecy was born from his memory.
